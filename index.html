01 — Introduction

About Myself

🎓SLIIT Undergraduate
💼Full-Stack Developer
🤖AI/ML Research
☁️Cloud Technologies

My name is Shashini Nathasha, and I am currently an undergraduate student following the Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Information Technology degree at the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT). I am passionate about software engineering, modern web technologies, and intelligent systems development.

I have hands-on experience in both frontend and backend software development. My technical skills include React, Next.js, Spring Boot, Node.js, Express.js, Tailwind CSS, MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Docker, Redis, GitHub, and Figma. I enjoy building responsive and user-friendly applications while also working on scalable backend architectures.

During my internship at Sri Lanka Telecom (SLT), I collaborated with agile teams on frontend and backend tasks. Currently, I work as a Full-Stack Developer at ClockworksCode, contributing to Next.js and Spring Boot microservices-based products.

I also contributed to the CinnamonSri AI project — an intelligent system for the Sri Lankan cinnamon industry featuring quality prediction, disease detection, price forecasting, and marketplace recommendations. My future goal is to become a skilled software engineer and AI engineer building scalable, impactful solutions.
